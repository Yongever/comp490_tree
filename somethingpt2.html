DBH 2019 refers to the diameter at breast height (in inches) measured in 2019. Example: 10.5 inches.
DBH 2022 refers to the diameter at breast height (in inches) measured in 2022. Example: 12.3 inches.
Combine the prefix and number to form the full species code. Example: PRSE + 1 = PRSE1.
Choose the correct Latin name of the tree. Example: Quercus alba for White Oak.
Select the common name of the tree. Example: White Oak.
Choose the hazard rating of the tree. Example: Healthy, Extreme distress, or Some distress.
